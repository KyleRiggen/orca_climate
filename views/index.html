  • Orca Climate Fund

    Orca Climate Fund (previously named the Impact Fund) represents the Orca’s commitment to positive impact on the wider world beyond crypto. A portion of trading fees on Orca goes into the fund, which is then used to contribute to climate change mitigation efforts and sustainability.

  • About the Climate Fund

    Mission:

    The Orca Climate Fund exists to provide climate-tech entrepreneurs with funding to build, innovate, and iterate towards restoring and preserving Earth health from ocean to ozone.

    Vision:

    Our vision of the future is one where innovative organizations play a vital role in promoting a healthy and abundant Earth. We believe that 'unicorns' should be measured not just by their sales, but also by their commitment to planetary regeneration.

    Values:

    Transparency: We strive to be transparent in our investments, decision-making, and reporting of impact.

    Financial sustainability: We manage finances for long-term effectiveness and find sustainable solutions that are financially and ecologically sound.

    Holistic Web3: We strive to bridge gap between on-chain and off-chain, between science and technology, between sustainability and Web3

    Measurable Impact: We aim to achieve measurable impact to ensure environmental outcomes.

    Access: We aim to democratize access to funding and resources to catalyze action in areas with the greatest need.

    Areas of Interest:

    Ecosystem Health Monitoring: This includes the regular monitoring of the health of ecosystems, such as forests, oceans, and wetlands, to ensure that they are not being negatively impacted by human activities. It also involves developing and implementing strategies to mitigate any negative impacts that are identified.

    Habitat and Biodiversity Preservation and Regeneration: It is important to preserve natural habitats and the biodiversity that they support, as they are essential for the health of ecosystems and the survival of many species. This involves identifying areas that are at risk of degradation or destruction and taking steps to protect them, as well as restoring areas that have already been impacted by human activities.

    Supply Chain and Materials Management: Ensuring that the materials used in production are sustainable and responsibly sourced is critical to reducing the environmental impact of business operations. This involves developing and implementing supply chain management strategies that prioritize sustainability and responsible sourcing. It also includes the responsible management of materials, such as reducing waste and recycling materials whenever possible.

    De-Carbonization: The importance of removing greenhouse gases from the atmosphere and moving towards renewable, less polluting norms for industry cannot be overstated. Greenhouse gas emissions are a major contributor to climate change and the resulting damage to our planet's ecosystems, which has profound implications for our health, our economies, and our way of life. Investing in and supporting companies that prioritize the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions is crucial to mitigating the worst effects of climate change and ensuring a sustainable future for generations to come.
